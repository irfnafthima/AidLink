Emergency Guides

Expert protocols and safety instructions for critical situations.

Fire Emergency

  • Stay low to the ground.
  • Use stairs, never elevators.
  • Check doors for heat.

Medical First Aid

  • Call for ambulance first.
  • Check for breathing/pulse.
  • Apply pressure to bleeding.

Safety & Security

  • Find a safe, locked room.
  • Keep your phone silent.
  • Avoid any confrontation.

Natural Disasters

  • Move to higher ground.
  • Stockpile bottled water.
  • Monitor radio alerts.
